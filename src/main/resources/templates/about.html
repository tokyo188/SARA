About Resource Finder and Donator

Our Mission

Resource Finder and Donator is a comprehensive platform designed to connect volunteers, donors, and communities during disasters and urgent needs. Our mission is to streamline the process of resource allocation and volunteer coordination to ensure help reaches those who need it most.

What We Do

  • Connect people who need resources with those who can provide them
  • Coordinate volunteers with skills matching community needs
  • Facilitate donations of money, goods, and services
  • Provide real-time updates on resource availability
  • Track and manage resource distribution

How It Works

  1. Register as a volunteer, donor, or resource seeker
  2. Browse available resources or post new ones
  3. Connect with volunteers in your area
  4. Coordinate with donors for specific needs
  5. Track resource status and updates

Technology Stack

This application is built using modern web technologies:

  • Backend: Spring Boot 3.2.0 with Java 17
  • Frontend: Thymeleaf templates with Bootstrap 5
  • Database: H2 in-memory database
  • Build Tool: Maven
  • Data Access: Spring Data JPA
